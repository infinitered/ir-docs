# About Infinite Red

Infinite Red is a team of down-to-earth designers, engineers, and teachers, helping connect dots and push tech forward. We’re just a few humble humans (and cats) using React Native to help people get things off the ground and out into the world.

A few faces on our team might seem familiar to you. We’ve been in this game for a long time, and we’ve got the happy clients and ever-evolving community to prove it.
